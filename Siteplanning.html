Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the White Water Rafting website is to build revenue by promoting thrilling rafting adventures, providing detailed trip information, and enabling online bookings to attract adventure seekers and families looking for exciting outdoor experiences.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts aged 18–45, including families and groups, with moderate to high disposable income, who seek thrilling outdoor activities. They are interested in nature, physical challenges, and memorable group experiences. Their needs include clear trip details, safety assurances, and easy booking processes, which are not always met by competitors’ vague or outdated websites. The audience primarily accesses the site via mobile devices (60%) and laptops (40%) for planning and booking trips.
